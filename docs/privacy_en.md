# Privacy Policy - Unforgettable Voyages

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

The privacy of our users is our priority. This document explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## 1. What data do we collect?
1.1. **Account data:** E-mail address, username (nickname).
1.2. **App data:** Travel plans, costs, visited places, profile photos.
1.3. **Technical data:** Device model, OS version, anonymous usage statistics.

## 2. Purpose of data processing
2.1. Providing travel planning services.
2.2. Synchronizing data between the user's devices.
2.3. Personalizing the user experience.
2.4. Handling payments for Premium services (via Google Play/App Store).

## 3. Storage and Security
3.1. Your data is stored securely in the Supabase database.
3.2. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.
3.3. We use connection encryption (SSL) and modern database security standards.

## 4. Data Sharing
4.1. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
4.2. Data may be shared with service providers (e.g., Supabase, RevenueCat) solely for the correct operation of the App.

## 5. User Rights
5.1. You have the right to access your data, rectify it, restrict its processing, and permanently delete your account and data (the right to be forgotten).
5.2. You have the right to data portability, allowing you to obtain and reuse your data across different services.
5.3. You have the right to object to the processing of your data and the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.
5.4. You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time, which involves deleting your account.

## 6. Cookies and tracking technologies
6.1. The app may use analytical tools (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) to monitor errors and stability.

## 7. Contact
If you have questions about your privacy, write to us: [unforgettablevoyages@protonmail.com](mailto:unforgettablevoyages@protonmail.com)
